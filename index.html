We are a group hoping to achieve

A better insight on the needed academic foundations for a student

Our civics project, known as StudentAcademicSuccess (SAS), harnesses the power of social media posts, podcasts, and interviews to raise awareness and promote action in advocating for enhanced academic support for struggling students. Through engaging content and thought-provoking discussions, SAS aims to inform and empower teachers, students, and adults alike to play an active role in addressing academic challenges. By leveraging these digital platforms, we foster dialogue, share resources, and inspire advocacy efforts to ensure that every student receives the necessary support to thrive academically. Together, through SAS, we work towards building a community committed to the success and well-being of every learner.
